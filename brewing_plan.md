## 📝 Quick Recipe (Blonde Ale — ~5% ABV)

### Malt Bill (for ~20L batch)
| Ingredient | Amount |
|------------|--------|
| Pale malt (2-row) | 4.5 kg |
| Carapils / Carafoam | 0.3 kg (body + head retention) |
| Munich malt | 0.2 kg (complexity) |

### Hops
| Addition | Amount | Time | Purpose |
|----------|--------|------|---------|
| Cascade or Saaz | 25g | 60min (boil) | Bitterness |
| Cascade | 15g | 10min (boil) | Flavour |
| Cascade | 10g | Flameout / whirlpool | Aroma |

### Yeast
| Type | Amount | Note |
|------|--------|------|
| US-05 (dry) | 1 sachet (11g) | Rehydrate or pitch dry — reliable, clean |

### Process Summary
1. **Mash** at 66°C for 60 min (ratio ~3L water per kg grain)
2. **Sparge** with 75°C water to collect ~23L pre-boil
3. **Boil** 60 min, add hops per schedule
4. **Chill** to 20°C (use wort chiller or ice bath)
5. **Pitch** US-05 yeast
6. **Ferment** at 18–20°C for 10 days
7. **Keg + carbonate** July 14–17
8. **Serve** July 18

## ⚠️ Critical Notes

- **This is a tight timeline.** 14 days from grain to glass is doable with US-05, but there's no buffer. Brew MUST happen July 4.
- **Temperature control matters.** Keep fermenter at 18–20°C (not higher — off-flavours). A basement or cool room is fine.
- **Sanitize everything.** With a short turnaround, there's no time for infections to show before serving.
- **If brew day can't happen July 4:** Switch to a commercial keg purchase plan (₪150–250 for a 20L keg from a local brewery or distributor).
- **Backup option:** If brewing doesn't work out, buy kegged beer from mini-brewery (e.g., Beer Merchant, Dancing Camel, etc.) — budget ~₪200-300.
